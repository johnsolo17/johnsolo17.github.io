John Sorgen

I am an eager designer with an understanding of programming languages and a passion for creating intuitive and user-friendly websites.



My UX Skills

User Research

User Research allows me to gain a deeper understanding of my target audience and make informed design decisions based on their needs, behaviors, and preferences. For my projects, I have conducted surveys, interviews, and usability tests to gather data and gain insight into the behavior of a user.

User research enabled me to validate design assumptions, identify areas for improvement, and create more effective, user-centered designs. My experience with user research has also helped to build empathy with my users, and to design products that truly meet their needs. Overall, incorporating user research into my projects as a UX/UI Designer is a key element in creating successful, user-centered designs.

User Interface Design

Designing user interfaces and prototyping designs has been a core aspect of my recent work. I continue to develop a keen eye for clean, intuitive, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces that are easy for users to navigate. I understand the importance of considering the user’s needs, behaviors, and feedback throughout the design process, and use this knowledge to inform my design decisions.

In my work, I have used a variety of design tools and software, such as Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, and Photoshop, to create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes of my designs. I’ve also created interactive, high-fidelity representations of my designs, allowing me to test and refine my ideas before they are fully developed. This experience has helped me to create user-centered solutions that meet the needs of my target audience and deliver a positive user experience.

Web Development

As a UX/UI Designer, learning HTML and CSS has been a valuable step in improving my overall skills as a designer. HTML and CSS are the building blocks of the web, and having a good understanding of these technologies has enabled me to create more informed and effective designs.

Through my learning experience, I have gained a deeper understanding of how web pages are structured and how to create designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and accessible to all users. My knowledge of HTML and CSS has enabled me to communicate more effectively with front-end developers and to work more closely with them in the implementation of my designs.

Additionally, learning HTML and CSS has allowed me to expand my skillset and become a more versatile designer, capable of working on a wider range of projects.
